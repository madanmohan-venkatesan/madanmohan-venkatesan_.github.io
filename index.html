About me

I am a Site Reliability engineer with a passion of democratizing the data and making AI accessible to everyone. I believe that data and AI together are the most powerful tool to change the way of automating time-consuming tedious tasks that allows humans to focus on more important and creative works.

I started my career as a database administrator progressing slowly into data engineering domain that led me to pursue a career in the field of reliability engineering. My core skills are build, run and maintain database infrastructure, My machine learning skills include data analysis, Predictive modelling, cluster modelling, recommender system modelling and productionizing GenAI apps.

Over the years, I have developed strong foundation in problem solving, project management. I often work on end-to-end deployments of machine learning projects from prototyping to implementing MLOps in the existing systems.

You can look at some of project that I have created during my spare time. I will link my work to their GitHub repositories, feel free to download and play around with it.
